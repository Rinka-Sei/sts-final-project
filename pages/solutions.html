Development of Ethical Frameworks

IEEE Global Initiative on Ethics

The IEEE Global Initiative on Ethics of Autonomous and Intelligent Systems has developed the "Ethically Aligned Design" framework, which provides guidance on embedding ethics into the design of AI systems.

European Commission's Ethics Guidelines

The European Commission's "Ethics Guidelines for Trustworthy AI" provides a comprehensive framework for developers to ensure the ethical use of AI.

Responsible AI Practices

Microsoft's Responsible AI Practices

Microsoft's Responsible AI practices focus on transparency, fairness, accountability, and inclusiveness in AI development.

Google's AI Principles

Google's AI Principles emphasize the development of AI that is socially beneficial, avoids bias, and is accountable.

Policy Recommendations

OECD AI Principles

The OECD AI Principles provide a set of guidelines for the responsible development and deployment of AI, including recommendations for policy and regulatory frameworks.

EU's White Paper on Artificial Intelligence

The EU's White Paper on Artificial Intelligence outlines policy options for promoting the development and adoption of AI while addressing potential risks.

Collaborative Initiatives

Partnership on AI (PAI)

The Partnership on AI (PAI) is a consortium of tech companies, non-profits, and academics working together to address ethical challenges in AI (Source: Partnership on AI. "About the Partnership on AI.").
